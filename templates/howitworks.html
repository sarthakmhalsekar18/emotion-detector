How EmotionAI Works

Discover the technology behind our advanced emotion recognition system

The Science of Emotion Recognition

Our EmotionAI system uses deep learning to analyze facial expressions and detect human emotions with remarkable accuracy. The technology is based on decades of psychological research combined with cutting-edge artificial intelligence.

1

Face Detection

The system first identifies and locates human faces in the input image using advanced computer vision algorithms. It can detect multiple faces in a single image and works with various angles and lighting conditions.

2

Feature Extraction

Key facial landmarks (eyes, eyebrows, nose, mouth) are identified and measured. The system analyzes subtle changes in these features that correspond to different emotional states.

3

Emotion Classification

A deep neural network processes the facial features and classifies the expression into one of seven universal emotions: happiness, sadness, anger, surprise, fear, disgust, or neutral.

4

Result Delivery

The detected emotion is displayed along with confidence scores.Here the algorithm used is Federated Learning The system provides clear, easy-to-understand results while maintaining the privacy of your data.
